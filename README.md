# FAST - Ivo Mendes
This repository documents my work as a member of the Electrical Systems Department at FEUP Academic Solar Team (FAST), a multidisciplinary student project at Faculdade de Engenharia da Universidade do Porto focused on developing a solar-powered autonomous vessel for European university competitions.
My work within the team spans electrical system design, power distribution architecture, component selection, and hardware integration — with a particular focus on ensuring reliable operation of sensors, actuators, and autonomous hardware in a maritime environment.
